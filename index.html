Welcome to Our School

Life Academy School is located in Pakhanjore, a town in the Kanker district of Chhattisgarh, India. Since our founding, we have been committed to providing quality education that combines strong academics, moral values, and co-curricular growth for every student.

About Our School

Life Academy School was established with a simple mission: to provide every child in Pakhanjore and the surrounding villages with access to good quality, affordable education. Over the years, we have grown into one of the trusted schools in the region.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops the intellectual, physical, social, and moral capabilities of every student, preparing them to become responsible citizens of India.

Our Vision

To be recognised as a centre of academic excellence in Kanker district, known for discipline, innovation, and care for every learner.

Academics

We follow a curriculum affiliated with the state education board, covering classes from Nursery to Class 12. Subjects are taught by qualified and experienced teachers, using both traditional and technology-supported teaching methods.
